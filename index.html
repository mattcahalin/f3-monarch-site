The 3 F's

F3 stands on three core pillars. Everything we do flows from these.

Fitness

Free, intense, peer-led workouts at 5:15 AM. Every workout is functional, scalable, and led by a different man each time. No two are alike.

Fellowship

Men holding each other accountable, on the field and off. The sweat fades, the bonds don't. This is where real friendships are built.

Faith

A calling to lead and serve — in our homes, workplaces, and communities. F3 is faith-friendly to all men, regardless of background or belief.
